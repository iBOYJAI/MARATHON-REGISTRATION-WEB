About Tamil Marathon

A Celebration of Culture, Fitness, and Community

Our Mission

The Tamil Marathon is more than just a race—it's a movement to celebrate Tamil culture, promote health and fitness, and bring communities together. Held annually in the vibrant city of Chennai, this event attracts thousands of runners from across India and around the world.

Our mission is to inspire people of all ages and backgrounds to embrace an active lifestyle while honoring the rich heritage of Tamil Nadu. Through this marathon, we support local initiatives focused on education, healthcare, and cultural preservation.
